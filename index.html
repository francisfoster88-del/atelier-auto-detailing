FAQ

Do you need access to water and power?

For the best results, yes. If you don’t have either, message us and we’ll tell you what’s possible.

How long does it take?

Most jobs are 2–3 hours. Signature details can be 3–5 hours depending on condition and vehicle size.

Why “from” prices?

Every car is different. Photos let us price fairly (vehicle size, pet hair, heavy soiling, etc.).

Do you offer maintenance plans?

Yes. Monthly or fortnightly plans are great for busy owners — ask us for options.
